Hello fellow earth being.

Every moment we spend breathing is the outcome of roughly 14 billion years of evolution. Zoom out far enough and insignificance is unavoidable. But the fact that this process produced a human brain capable of asking questions and trying to understand the universe is genuinely astonishing. Adding to that somehow matrix multiplication has led to mimicking intelligence and that fascinates me every single day.
